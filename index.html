Frequently Asked Questions

What is the public transportation system?

The public transportation system includes buses, jeepneys, and tricycles operated by local authorities.

How can I access healthcare services?

You can visit your local barangay health center or hospital for medical assistance.

How do I apply for government education services?

You can apply through your local public school or online portals for scholarship programs.
