Educational Tips

Create a quiet and organized study space. Set specific goals for each study session and take short breaks to stay focused.

Use a planner or digital calendar to allocate time for studies, extracurriculars, and relaxation.

Read daily, even for a few minutes. Summarize what you’ve read to boost retention and understanding.
