Tips to Prevent Heat Stroke

Drink plenty of water throughout the day. Avoid sugary, caffeinated, and alcoholic beverages as they can dehydrate you.

Stay indoors during the hottest parts of the day (10 AM - 4 PM). If you must go outside, wear a hat and lightweight, light-colored clothing.

Recognize early symptoms of heat exhaustion, such as dizziness, fatigue, and excessive sweating, and take action immediately.
